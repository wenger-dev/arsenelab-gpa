How to Use the GPA Calculator

Our free GPA calculator is designed specifically for students in Rwanda and worldwide. It supports both weighted and unweighted GPA calculations, making it perfect for university and college students.

  • Enter your subject name (e.g., Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry)
  • Input your score (0-100) based on your institution's grading scale
  • Add credit units if available (optional) for weighted GPA calculation
  • Click "Add Subject" to include it in your calculation
  • Click "Calculate GPA" when you're ready to see your results
